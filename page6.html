FAQs

Do you deliver to all districts in Brunei?

Sorry no, we only do deliveries in Brunei-Muara District.

Is there any charges for delivery?

There will be charges of B$2 for orders below B$15. However for orders that exceed B$15, there will be free delivery.

How long does it take to deliver?

It depends on the location where our customers are located at. Since we're located in Kiarong, it will probably take around 20-30 minutes to arrive to the areas near by us such as Beribi and Kiulap. For locations that are further. it will probably take around 30-40 minutes to arrive. 

What is your business hours?

Our business hours is from 9AM to 9PM and we accept orders during that time duration. 

Will customers be notified upon the confirmation of order?

Yes, customers will be notified through e-mail or via SMS to the mobile phone provided by customers through the order form.

What are the methods of ordering?

Customers can choose to order through filling in our order form online or through direct phone call with the phone number provided by us.
